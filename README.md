# Scout 15 RPAS — Public Compliance Record

This repository is the public compliance and audit record for the **Scout 15**,
a small remotely piloted aircraft system (RPAS) operated in Canada under Part IX
of the Canadian Aviation Regulations (CARs).

## Why this repository exists

It provides a transparent, verifiable evidence trail for the aircraft's operation,
so that Transport Canada — or any interested party — can review how the Scout 15
and its operator meet the requirements applicable to their RPAS operations,
including the Standard 922 safety-assurance evidence for advanced operations.

## What it contains

The completed records supporting the Scout 15's operation:

- **Standard 922 Safety Assurance Declarations** and supporting evidence
  (e.g. operations in controlled airspace and near people).
- **Operating manual** and standard operating procedures.
- **RPAS configuration** — the aircraft's hardware, firmware, and parameters.
- **Maintenance records** — inspections, servicing, and airworthiness history.
- **Flight records** — logged test and operational flights.

Documents are added as they are finalized; each reflects the configuration and
procedures actually used in operation.

- **Model:** Scout 15 (quadcopter; small RPA, 250 g–25 kg)
